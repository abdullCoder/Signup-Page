@ AJIN KOYON CANVA KYAUTA

Barka da zuwa shafin mu
na Rigistan ajin koyan Canva Kyauta.

Mun ji dadin yadda kuke sha'awar shiga ajin mu na Canva Kyauta , inda zamu dauke ku daga sifili zuwa jarumai wajen samar da hotuna masu kayatarwa ta amfani da Canva, duk a cikin harshen Hausa.

Game da Class:

Game da Class: An ƙirƙiri ajin mu na Canva don taimaka muku ƙaddamar da kerawa da ƙwarewar fasahar zane tare da Canva. Ko kai mafari ne ko kuma kana da ɗan gogewa a baya, wannan ajin a buɗe yake ga duk matakan fasaha.

Me Yasa Muke Bukatar Bayananku:

Don tabbatar da mafi kyawun ƙwarewar koyo a gare ku da kuma ci gaba da sabunta ku game da ajin, muna buƙatar wasu mahimman bayanai da kyau. Za a adana bayanan ku na sirri kuma a yi amfani da su kawai don manufar wannan ajin. Da fatan za a ɗauki ɗan lokaci don kammala wannan fom ɗin rajista don tabbatar da matsayin ku a cikin aji.

Muna sa ran fara wannan tafiya ta kirkira tare da ku!

Gaisuwa mafi kyau,
